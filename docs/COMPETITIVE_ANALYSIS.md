# Competitive analysis

## Method and limitation

The public Superteam track page and its exposed public-submissions feed were checked during initial project research. At that time, no public submissions were returned, so this document does not attribute claims to unverified competitors. It records the product differentiation criteria used for the build.

## Common category risks

- Generic backtesters that only chart historical yield.
- Raw odds alerts with no source or event verification.
- Generic AI betting bots with opaque recommendations.
- Market-maker demos that do not surface data reliability.
- Polished dashboards that lack a reproducible decision trail.

## TxLINE Sentinel differentiation

1. Separates operational alerts from market signals.
2. Correlates only confirmed events available by decision time, distinguishes post-event reaction from late confirmation, and explicitly labels unexplained movement.
3. Shows duplicate, out-of-order, sequence-gap, delayed, stale, and recovery behavior in the demo itself.
4. Uses deterministic templates rather than a required paid LLM.
5. Adds risk-capped paper settlement, settled-equity drawdown, and retained-movement counterfactual horizons to the same audit chain.

The strategy is to demonstrate trustworthy decision support rather than claim predictive profit.

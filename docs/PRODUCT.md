# Product

## One-sentence pitch

TxLINE Sentinel verifies, explains, causally associates, audits, and measures football market movements before any simulation-only paper decision.

> **SIMULATION ONLY — NO REAL MONEY**

## Problem

Professional market users can observe an odds change without knowing whether it is meaningful, whether a confirmed sporting event available at decision time explains it, whether the feed is stale or delayed, or whether the decision can be reproduced and evaluated later.

## Solution

The agent operates on transport-independent domain models. It:

1. validates provider inputs and ordering;
2. separates book percentage from classical overround and computes margin-adjusted normalized probabilities;
3. detects material movements against typed thresholds and rolling context;
4. associates only confirmed events whose source and received times satisfy causal gates;
5. reports current feed health separately from historical operational alerts;
6. emits deterministic explanations and a componentized Rule-based confidence score;
7. opens only risk-capped virtual positions;
8. evaluates retained movement at 30, 60, and 300 seconds;
9. settles from confirmed full-time/cancellation state and measures virtual analytics;
10. preserves the decision chain in a run-scoped append-only audit log.

## Users

- Sports trading and risk teams.
- Market operators and data providers.
- Quantitative analysts who need deterministic replay.
- B2B operations users who need feed-health and audit visibility.

## Interpretation

Normalized probability is a market-comparison measure, not a true outcome probability. The Rule-based confidence score is a deterministic heuristic, not a calibrated probability. Signal precision is short-horizon movement persistence, not evidence of profitable forecasting.

## Non-goals

TxLINE Sentinel has no real-money execution, wallet custody, bookmaker account integration, deposit or withdrawal path, profit promise, or martingale strategy.
